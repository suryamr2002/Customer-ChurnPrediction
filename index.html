🔍 Customer Churn Prediction using XGBoost

This project uses customer data, consumption history, and pricing patterns to predict churn in the energy sector. The model is built with XGBoost and shows high accuracy in identifying customers at risk.

📈 Project Highlights

  • Feature engineering based on energy consumption and pricing history
  • XGBoost model with hyperparameter tuning using GridSearchCV
  • ROC-AUC analysis, feature importance, and business insights
